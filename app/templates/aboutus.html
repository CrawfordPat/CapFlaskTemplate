Idiot's Guide to Textbook Money

When I started this project, I wanted to create a place where journalists, reserchers, and normal people could easily see how well their local public schools are funded. An emphasis of transperancy was critical to ensuring our mission was accomplished effectively; it's why we made the data open-source and decentralized (i.e wiki-school-funding)


How to use this website:

Go to the "Map" tab to see each state. Click on a state to see the state's public school funding information. Each state as a detail summary of how much funding is being spent on public schools per student.
